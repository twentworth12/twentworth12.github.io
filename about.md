I'm Tom Wentworth, CMO at [incident.io](https://incident.io). We build the incident response and on-call platform that engineering teams actually like using.

Before incident.io, I spent nearly five years as CMO at [Recorded Future](https://recordedfuture.com), where we grew the business from $90M to over $350M in ARR. Earlier stops: CMO at RapidMiner, SVP Product Marketing at Acquia, and — a long time ago — a software engineer who learned to code on a TI-99/4a. I've been an engineer-turned-CMO for most of my career, and I think it's the most useful kind of marketer to be right now.

This blog is where I write about building modern marketing teams: what's actually working, what isn't, and how AI is rewriting the playbook. The question I'm most obsessed with these days: how small a marketing team can you run if every person on it is leveraged with AI? At incident.io we're a team of 5, and most of my time goes into building the systems — a centralized GTM-context library, a swarm of agents, even a custom AI CMO — that let a small team punch well above its weight.

My rule of thumb for marketing in 2026: **Marketing Output = AI × Taste²**. AI is a multiplier; taste compounds.

I also host the [Scaleup Marketing](https://open.spotify.com/show/4ZP6kmJc6bJuXvmWvjP2Fl) podcast, where I interview marketers I want to learn from.
