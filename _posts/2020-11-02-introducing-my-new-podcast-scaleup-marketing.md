Today I launched a new podcast. You're thinking just what we need another podcast, right? Okay, fair enough.

But I'm trying to fill a gap. While there are lots of marketing podcasts that focus on hot takes, opinions, and startups, I couldn't really find anything that was specific to the challenges I faced as the [CMO of a \$100m+ scale-up company](https://recordedfuture.com). So I started one.

I'm going to interview people who I think can make me a better marketing leader, and I hope they will do the same for anyone who listens. I'm going to publish a new episode every two weeks to start.

First up is [Dave Kellogg](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kelloggdave/), a Silicon Valley legend with 10+ years experience at each of the CEO, CMO, and board levels across ten different companies ranging in size from \$0 to over \$1B in revenues. When I was first getting into marketing about a decade ago, I binge read everything Dave wrote about marketing [on his pioneering blog https://kellblog.com](https://kellblog.com).
