Mercedes focuses on executive-level search for high-growth technology companies, with deep expertise in B2B SaaS companies. Mercedes and I talk about the role of executive search and how she identifies and qualifies marketing candidates. And we talk about the diversity problem in tech and the role executive recruiters play in helping solve it.
